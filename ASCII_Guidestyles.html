3. URL Encoding Visualization

Before Encoding:

https://example.com/search?query=Madhav Gautam&email=madhav@gmail.com

After Encoding:

https://example.com/search?query=Madhav%20Gautam&email=madhav%40gmail.com

Some common encoded characters:

  • Space --> %20
  • & --> %26
  • = --> %3D
  • ? --> %3F

4. Guide Style

❌Messy HTML:

<DIV><IMG src=cat.jpg><h1>Welcome</H1></DIV>

✅Fixed HTML (Guide Style):

<div>
  <img src="cat.jpg" alt="cute cat" />
  <h1>Welcome</h1>
</div>
